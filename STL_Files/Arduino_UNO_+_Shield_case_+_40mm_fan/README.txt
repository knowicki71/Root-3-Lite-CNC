# Summary

Case for arduino UNO + Shield. 

I use it for my CNC shield so, I added a 40mm fan to keep it cool enough.

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: RepRap
Printer: Prusa I3
Supports: No
Resolution: 0.2 mm
Infill: 25%

Notes: 
Printed in ABS.
Enable cooling in order to keep vertical walls as straight as possible.
To keep all parts as flat as possible, I always use a brim of 5 mm and I let all the parts cooling slowly before scrap it from the bed.
